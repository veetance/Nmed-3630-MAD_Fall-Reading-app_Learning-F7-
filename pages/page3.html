Future of computing

Quantum computing is the process of taking advantage of the ability of particles to exist in more than one state at the same time—such as being both an electron and a photon—for calculations. Today's most advanced computers use bits (which are either 0 or 1) to represent information in binary code. However, qubits can be 0 and 1 at once, which allows them to perform tasks much faster than regular computers because they can process multiple operations simultaneously instead of just one at a time like regular ones do.
